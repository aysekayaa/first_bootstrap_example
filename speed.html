What is Speed?

The movement of something is called speed. cars, people, animals, etc. The speed of things (animate or inanimate) specify the measurement of how fast or slow something moves.

Common units of speed

  1. Kilometer per hour (km/h).
  2. Miles per hour (mi/h or mph).
  3. Knot(Kt)
  4. Mach (M or Ma)
